about me

Hello, I'm Julie! I am an outrageously passionate recent user experience graduate currently seeking a UX designer or researcher position in the Seattle area. I am fascinated by people and how technology can support even the most technophobic among us. Incurably curious and a natural communicator, I love observing people, discerning patterns, and helping to inform design with true stories about users.

I just completed my master’s in HCI at City University of London. Before entering the UX field, I was an engineer designing hardware, software, and requirements in the sports and medical device industries. This background has given me invaluable insight into the practical aspects of “how things are made”, a strong analytical problem-solving ability, and a secret love for programming (I dabble in HTML/CSS, SQL, Processing, and C).

my services

what i do

user research

Getting to know users however possible: observations, surveys, interviews... NOTE: I don't actually use binoculars. That would be creepy.

interaction design

Wireframes, user flows, customer journey maps...let's make the product better! How? Iterate, iterate, iterate.

prototype

From paper to animated simulations, my favorite way to test out a real experience with the design is to make a prototype!

usability evaluation

How do I know if my design actually made things better? Watching people struggle with poorly-designed technology is painful but enlightening.
